Pairwise Comparison Instructions


On the next page, you will explore an interactive visualization.


Task:

Your task is to rank however many colleges provided you want to, with the "best" on top. You will be provided a ranking based off your input. Afterwards, you will be asked to describe some of your findings, and answer a few questions on the understanding of the visualization.

How to Operate the Build Page:

>> Mouseover any of the colleges to obtain more details.


>> Drag colleges from the dataset on the left to form pairs on the right

>> Each pair needs two colleges, with your more preferred college on the left

>> Each college can be used in multiple pairs


>> Click “sort” button to order the colleges alphabetically"

>> Click “shuffle” button to randomize which colleges you see

>>Use search bar to search for specific colleges.


>> Navigate to your offical ranking by clicking "RANK!"

How to Operate the Explore Page:

>> When exploring your final ranking scroll horizonatally or vertially for more information

>> Look through pages of results,and click "Edit Preferences" to go back and change your preferences
