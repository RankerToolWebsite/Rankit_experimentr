Categorical Comparison Instructions


On the next page, you will use an interactive tool to create a personalized College Ranking


Task:

Your task is to rank as many of the provided colleges as desired, with the each college being placed in a category. Afterwards, the system will provide you with a ranking of the entire list based on your initial choices.

At that point, you may click Edit Preferences to return to the Build page and refine your choices. When you are finished with the ranking tool, you will be asked to describe some of your findings, and answer a few questions on your experience with this interactive rank building system.

How to Operate the Build Page:

>> Mouse over an individual college to see its data attributes.


>> Drag colleges from the dataset to the left to place them in a category: “High”, “Medium” or “Low”

>> Place colleges in categories that correspond to your preference


>> Click “sort” button to order the colleges alphabetically"

>> Click “shuffle” button to randomize which colleges you see

>>Use search bar to search for specific colleges.


How to Operate the Explore Page:

>> Navigate to your system-generated ranking of the remaining items by clicking "RANK!"


>> When exploring your final ranking, you may scroll down for more colleges in the list, and scroll to the right for more information on a particular college, as shown in the table. You also may look through multiple pages of the results.

>> If you want to refine your choices and obtain a new system-generated ranking, click "Edit Preferences" to go back to the Build tool.


You will not be able to return to this page
