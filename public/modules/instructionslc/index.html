Instructions


Please read the following instructions carefully. You will not be able to return to this page once you begin the experiment!

Task:

On the next page, you will use an interactive tool to create a personalized college ranking. First, you will use the Build Tool to enter your preferences about colleges in the dataset. Choose as many colleges as you wish and place each one into a ranked list. After you make your selections, the system will provide you with a ranking of the entire dataset of colleges based on your initial choices.

You may inspect your ranking and return to the Build page to refine your preferences as many times as you like. When you are finished with the ranking tool, you will be asked to describe your findings, and answer a few questions on your experience with this interactive rank building system.


How to Operate the Build Page:

  • Mouse over an individual college to see its data attributes.


  • Drag colleges from the dataset on the left to the list on the right. Place the most preferred colleges at the top of the list, and the rest in descending order of your preference.


  • Click the “sort” button to order the colleges alphabetically.

    Click the “shuffle” button to randomize which colleges you see.

    Use the search bar to search for specific colleges.


Viewing the Rank Explorer Page:

  • Navigate to your system-generated ranking of the remaining items by clicking "RANK!".



  • To exploring your ranking, scroll down for more colleges in the list, and scroll to the right for more information on a particular college. You also may look through multiple pages of the results.


  • If you want to refine your choices and obtain a new system-generated ranking, click "Edit Preferences" to go back to the Build tool.
